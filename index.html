LIFE
Your own creation
What is this?
This game is a zero-player game made by John Conway, a British Mathematician. However here, you have the right to manipulate the rules and create your own patterns, test your universe and see it growing or dying.
Welcome to the Rule Book of your universe
  • Any living cell with less than 2 neighbours dies.
  • Any living cell with more than 3 neighbours dies.
  • Any living cell with 2 neighbours survives to the next generation.
  • Any dead cell with 3 neighbours becomes alive.
